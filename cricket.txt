The bat was raised, the field was bright,
A cricket dream took morning flight.
The bowler ran with fire and grace,
The crowd stood still, heart full of pace.

The ball came fast, the willow sang,
Across the ground the echo rang.
A single run, then four, then more,
The fans erupted with a roar.

The captain smiled, the fielders chased,
No moment lost, no chance was wasted.
Under the sun, with hope and pride,
The game of cricket lived outside.

For every ball and every run,
There is a story, there is fun.
Win or lose, we still believe,
Cricket gives what hearts receive.
